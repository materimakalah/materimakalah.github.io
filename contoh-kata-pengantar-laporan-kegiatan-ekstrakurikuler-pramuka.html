20. Ceritakan tentang kegiatan ekstrakurikuler Pramuka di sekolah dalam bhs inggris ​


Jawaban:

Scouting or Pramuka is a popular extracurricular activity in schools throughout Indonesia. Pramuka is a youth movement that emphasizes character building, leadership skills, outdoor skills, and community service. Students who participate in Pramuka learn valuable skills and knowledge that they can use throughout their lives.

The Pramuka activities in schools usually involve outdoor activities such as camping, hiking, and other forms of adventure. These activities help students develop teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership skills. Students also learn how to survive in the wilderness and become more independent and self-reliant.

In addition to outdoor activities, Pramuka also includes community service projects. Students work together to plan and implement projects that benefit their communities, such as cleaning up public spaces, planting trees, or organizing blood donation drives. Through these projects, students learn the value of giving back to their communities and develop a sense of social responsibility.

One of the most important aspects of Pramuka is the Scout Promise and Law. This is a set of principles that Scouts pledge to live by, which include being trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent. These principles guide Scouts in their daily lives and help them become responsible and contributing members of society.

Overall, Pramuka is a fun and rewarding extracurricular activity that teaches students valuable skills and principles. It is a great way for students to develop their character, leadership, and teamwork skills, while also giving back to their communities.
