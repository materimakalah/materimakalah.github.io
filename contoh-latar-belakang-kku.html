15. Bantu dulu eee cari tugas bahasa inggris kku dong


Answer:

A dialogue is a conversation which happens between 2 people or more talking about a certain topic.

Here is the dialogue which can be made based of the situation above.

A: Good morning, A!

B: Good morning, B!

A: How was your holiday?

B: It was so great!

A: Really? what did you do during your holiday?

B: I went to Singapore with my family. Singapore is a beautiful and clean country. I went to many tourism objects, such as the Sentosa Island, Gardens by the bay, Singapore Botanic Gardens, the Night Safari, and the Orchard Road.

A: Wow, it sounds so fun.

B: Indeed. How about you? What did you do during holiday?

A: I spent my holiday in my grandparents' house. They live in a village which is quite distant from the city. They are farmers. I learnt a lot of things about farming from them.

B: So did you farm?

A: Of course. It was so tiring, you know. I had to plant the spinach one by one in the correct range. Moreover, i had to do it during mid day. That's why my skin becomes tan.

B: Wow, what a nice experience. How is the village?

A: It is so beautiful and calm. The air is very fresh there. It's very different from the city.

B: Really? It makes me interested to visit a village.

A: You can visit the village with me for the next holiday.

B: Are you sure?

A: Of course.

B: I will ask my parents later if that so.

A: Alright.

I hope the answer is clear enough and meets your expectation.

Ciri-ciri Perindustrian adalah:

Memiliki bahan bakuMemiliki pekerjaMemiliki MesinMemiliki Modal untuk membayar pengoperasian produksi

Perdagangan adalah suatu kegiatan ekonomi yang bertujuan untuk memasarkan produksi barang atau jasa ke pembeli, sehingga mengakibatkan proses tawar menawar.

Ciri-ciri Perdagangan adalah:  

Adanya Barang yang dipasarkanAdanya PembeliAdanya Penjual  Adanya alat transaksi, yaitu uangTerjadinya tawar-menawar harga barang antara pembeli dan penjualAdanya keuntungan dari barang yang dijual
