One day a cat and a fox were having a conversation. The fox, who was a conceited creature, boasted how clever she was. 'Why, I know at least a hundred tricks to get away from our mutual enemies, the rat,' she said.

'I know only one trick to get away from rat,' said the cat. 'You should teach me some of yours!'
'Well, maybe some day, when I have the time, I may teach you a few of the simpler ones,' replied the fox airily.

Just then they heard the barking of a pack of rat in the distance. The barking grew louder and louder - the rats were coming in their direction! At once the cat ran to the nearest tree and climbed into its branches, well out of reach of any rats. 'This is the trick I told you about, the only one I know,' said the cat. 'Which one of your hundred tricks are you going to use?'

The fox sat silently under the tree, wondering which trick she should use. Before she could make up her mind, the rats arrived. They fell upon the fox and tore her to pieces.

Moral : A single plan that works is better than a hundred doubtful plans.

22. kata konstitusi merupakan terjemahan dari bahasa inggris,yaitu?


Konstitusi  berasal dari kata constitution atau yang berarti sistem tata negara yang mengatur sebuah wilayah atau negara secara keseluruhan. Dengan adanya konstitusi, hukum tata negara dari sebuah negara akan lebih jelas dan negara memiliki sistem agar berjalan dengan lancar dan sesuai dengan aturannya.

Pembahasan

Sebuah negara pasti memiliki konstitusi atau dasar dalam tata negara yang mengatur bagaimana sebuah negara akan berjalan dengan ikatan aturan tertentu. Konstitusi  bisa diartikan sebagai dasar dan asas dari sebah negara dan kelompok tertentu. Konstitusi di perlukan untuk memudahkan penyelenggaraan negara dan juga sebuah wilayah.

Pada praktiknya, konstitusi merupakan kumpulan berbagai macam doktrin dan sistem penataan untuk negara dimana didalamnya terdapat berbagai maca aturan dan asas fundamental yang menentukan penyelenggaraan sebuah negara.

Di Indonesia sendiri, konstitusi dikenal dengan nama UUD atau Undang- Undang Dasar 1945, yang berisi segala peraturan fundamental penyelenggaraan Negara Kesatuan Republik Indonesia.

Jawaban, Konstitusi berasal dari kata constitution .

last holiday i visited my uncle's family who live in puncak. his house is near a tea plantation and has wonderful surroundings.
one morning, my cousin Gani, who is two years older than me, showed me his horse. Gani said that he is a pretty skillful horse rider. to hear that, i felt envious. so, i asked him to teach me how to ride a horse. Gani then asked me whether i was really brave enough to ride a horse. confidenty i said yes.
Gani taught me how to sit on the horse saddle and hold the horse's bridles. he didn't let me ride the horse by myself. he sat behind me and guided the horse.
the following day, i intended to ride the horse by myself. the horse was quite tame so Gani allowed me to. however, he still watched me. he followed me on another horse. i held the bridles and rode the horse carefully. finally, i succeeded in riding the horse around the plantation. i felt very happy. it was like a dream that i could finally ride a horse!
you know what! it is the best holiday i've ever had i promises Gani that i will return next holiday and explore the plantation with him, on horse. that'll be great!
