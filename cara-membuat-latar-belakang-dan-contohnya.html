- Simple random sampling

Simple random sampling atau pengambilan sampel secara acak merupakan metode di mana setiap unit sampel dari suatu populasi memiliki kesempatan yang sama untuk diikut sertakan dalam sampel.

merupakan pengambilan sampel dengan interval berkala tertentu. seperti pengambilan selisih antara dua bilangan berurutan atau celah tertentu. Adanya interval atau rumus tertentu untuk menentukan sampel ini yang membuat metode ini lebih sistematis.

The teaching and learning process is an activity that takes place by involving various components that interact with each other to achieve goals, one of which is student creativity. For this reason, it is necessary to make efforts to improve teaching that is more basic which concerns the problem solving process in the teaching and learning process to improve student achievement. The population is all students of class XI SMA 3 Jombang, while the samples taken are students of class XI-A. The research data were collected by test method, namely creativity test and problem solving ability test. The data were analyzed by simple regression correlation analysis. The results obtained are as follows. There is a positive and significant relationship between students' creativity on the ability to solve physics problems on the subject of physical optics with a correlation coefficient of r = 0.752 and a functional relationship is obtained between creativity and problem solving ability which is expressed by the line linear Y = 8.37 + 0.85X.
